World Affairs Conference

I'm the Director of Registration and Media for the World Affairs Conference: that means that I've designed the World Affairs Conference Website, as well as the conference signage, packages, and other media. The World Affairs Conference is a student-run current affairs conference held at Upper Canada College.

UCC Robotics

I'm the club head of UCC Robotics, and I've also designed the UCC Robotics Website. Visitors can learn about what our club is, and what it has to offer; in addition, club members can sign in to take attendance, as well as other small functions.

UCC Prefects Cup

I'm the web designer for the Prefects Cup Website, where UCC students can view live updates of the Prefects Cup standings, as well as upcoming events and schedules.
